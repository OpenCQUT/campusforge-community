# Governance

CampusForge uses a lightweight governance model for school internal communities.

## Roles

- **Student**: can apply for access and view own status.
- **Member**: can access resources, courses, and member directory.
- **Maintainer**: can manage assigned resources and courses.
- **Admin**: can review applications, manage policies, and audit activity.

## Policy lifecycle

1. Draft policy document.
2. Internal review by administrators.
3. Publish with `Active` status.
4. Review periodically.
5. Archive or replace when outdated.

## Invitation governance

Invitations are one-to-one, non-transferable, and reviewed by administrators. Application decisions should include documented reasoning and audit events.
